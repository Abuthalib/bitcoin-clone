Get started with Bitcoin

Bitcoin uses peer-to-peer technology to operate with
no central authority or banks; managing transactions
and the issuing of bitcoins is carried out collectively
by the network. Bitcoin is open-source; its design is public, nobody owns
or controls Bitcoin and everyone can take part Through many of its unique
properties, Bitcoin allows exciting uses that could not
be covered by any previous payment system.
